- Deep swimming overhaul — realistic, polished swim gameplay (request: "melhore profundamente o gameplay de nadar, deixe mais realista e polido" + "faça animações realistas para a natação e deixe bem polido"). The old swim was a vertical ped "walking in place" submerged to the chest (`animatePed` reused, fixed 3.2 speed, no inertia, no FX). Rebuilt it as a two-mode system in `js/player.js`:
  - **Two blended poses** via `player.swimPose` (0 = treading upright / 1 = prone front-crawl), eased on a lerp. Body posture set with Euler order `YXZ` so yaw steers the heading, then a forward pitch (up to ~64°) tips the body prone onto the surface, plus a small per-stroke roll (breathing). New `animateSwim()` drives the limbs (does NOT use `animatePed`): crawl = alternating over-the-top arm windmill with the elbow flexing on the underwater pull + a fast flutter kick; treading = arms sculling out wide + an alternating eggbeater/bicycle leg cycle. The windmill phase is wrapped to `[0,2π)` so a long swim doesn't leave the arms stuck at a huge `pose*sp` offset when you stop to tread (2π≡0 keeps the windmill itself seamless).
  - **Momentum + stroke-synced thrust**: own velocity (`swimVX/VZ`) with water drag and a top-speed clamp; each stroke delivers a propulsion PULSE (`pow(|sin(stroke)|,1.5)`, 2 per cycle) so you surge on the catch and glide between strokes instead of moving at constant speed. Heading turns smoothly toward the camera-relative input. Sprint (run) raises stroke cadence + thrust + top speed.
  - **Breath/stamina + drowning** (new `state.swimAir` 1→0): swimming drains air (sprint drains faster), it refills on land; at 0 the player starts taking drowning damage and `getWasted()` (wakes at the hospital). New `#breath` HUD bar (in `index.html` `#vc-stack`, styled in `css/style.css`, updated in `hud.js`) shows only while swimming/refilling and turns red + blinks when low.
  - **Water FX + audio**: new `splash(vol,big)` in `js/audio.js` (filtered-noise spray sweeping high→mid + a low "ploc" on `big`). Entry into water spawns a foam ring + big splash + slight shake and carries running momentum in; each stroke spits a foam jet at the lead hand with a stroke splash sound; a recycled foam-puff wake trail (reusing the boat's `spawnPuff`/`makeWakePuff` pool) follows the swimmer, denser when fast. Surface bob follows a gentle wave.
  - **Camera**: while swimming the chase cam lowers and pulls in (`dist 5.6`, `baseH 1.0`) and centers (no shoulder offset) for a water-skimming frame.
  - **Weapons holstered in water**: `weapons.js` (`updateWeapons`/`shootWeapon`/`isWeaponHeld`/`canAttack`) now gate on `state.swimming` so no held model, aim pose, crosshair, or firing happens while swimming (the swim pose owns the limbs); rampage rockets hide in water and resume on exit. `getWasted()` resets the YXZ swim posture before the death-fall animation so dying mid-swim looks normal.
  - Structure: `updateFoot` now early-returns into `updateSwim(dt)` when `inWater`; the old `if(swim){…}else{…}` land branch became an unconditional block; leaving water zeroes swim inertia, restores `XYZ` Euler order, and eases any residual body tilt to 0.
